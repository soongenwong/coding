About Our Club

We're a community of students passionate about AI, dedicated to exploring the incredible possibilities of building with Claude.

Are you a complete beginner curious about AI? Or an experienced developer looking to push the boundaries of what's possible with large language models? We have space for you! Our club provides a supportive environment to learn, experiment, and create amazing projects. Whether it's a chatbot, game, or app- we want to see it all!

What is Claude?

Claude is an AI tool developed by Anthropic. It assists in coding, writing, data analysis and other creative tasks.

At the heart of Anthropic's mission is AI safety and responsibility. AI should empower people to accomplish their goals more effectively while maintaining human agency and decision-making. Our club embraces these values, focusing on building applications that enhance human potential rather than creating dependency.

We're not just learning to use AI - we're learning to build with AI in ways that benefit everyone.
